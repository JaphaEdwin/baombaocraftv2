Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A standard kitchen takes 4-6 weeks from design approval to installation. Wardrobes typically take 2-3 weeks, while single furniture pieces may be completed in 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your quotation.

Do you provide design services?

Yes! Design is included in our process. We start with a consultation to understand your needs, then create detailed plans and, for larger projects, 3D visualizations before production begins.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin production, with the balance due upon completion before installation. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept mobile money, bank transfer, and cash.

Do you handle installation?

Absolutely. All our projects include professional installation by our trained team. We ensure everything fits perfectly and functions smoothly before final handover.

What warranty do you offer?

We stand behind our work with a 2-year warranty on craftsmanship and materials. This covers any manufacturing defects or issues arising from normal use. Hardware is covered by manufacturer warranties.

Can I visit your workshop?

Yes, we welcome workshop visits! It's a great way to see our materials, discuss your project, and understand our process. Contact us to schedule a convenient time.
