Our Story

Born From a Passion for Wood

BaoMbao Craft was founded in 2020 by master craftsman Samuel Bbosa, who trained under legendary woodworkers in both Uganda and Japan. The name "BaoMbao" comes from the Swahili word for "plank"—the fundamental building block of all our creations.

Today, our workshop in Kampala employs 12 master artisans, each specializing in different techniques from traditional joinery to modern CNC precision.
