Modern Kitchen Renovation

Kololo, Kampala

Project Overview

This Kololo residence underwent a complete kitchen transformation, replacing an outdated layout with a modern, functional space that maximizes storage and workflow while maintaining the warmth of natural wood.

The homeowners wanted a kitchen that could handle both everyday family cooking and occasional entertaining. We designed an L-shaped layout with a central island, creating clear zones for prep, cooking, and serving.

Working closely with the client, we selected Mahogany for its rich color and durability, finished with a semi-gloss lacquer that's both beautiful and easy to maintain. Premium Blum soft-close hardware ensures smooth, quiet operation for years to come.

Key Features

  • Custom Mahogany cabinetry with semi-gloss lacquer finish
  • Blum soft-close hinges and drawer systems
  • Integrated LED under-cabinet lighting
  • Pull-out pantry and corner carousel systems
  • Granite countertops with integrated sink
  • Custom-built breakfast island with storage

Our Process

How We Built It

01

Consultation & Design

We visited the home, assessed the existing layout, took detailed measurements, and discussed the family's needs. Our design team created 3D visualizations for client approval.

02

Material Selection

The client visited our workshop to select materials. They chose Mahogany for its warmth and durability, with brushed nickel handles for a modern touch.

03

Production

All cabinetry was precision-crafted in our workshop over three weeks. Each piece was quality-checked before finishing with semi-gloss lacquer.

04

Installation

Our team completed installation in 4 days, coordinating with the countertop supplier and electrician for seamless integration.
