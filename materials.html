Materials Library

We believe in material honesty — letting wood express its natural beauty. Explore our carefully selected wood species, finishes, and hardware options.

Foundation

Wood Species

We work with premium African hardwoods, each selected for its unique character, durability, and workability.

Musambya

Markhamia lutea

A Ugandan hardwood with beautiful golden-brown tones that deepen with age. Excellent durability and resistance to termites make it ideal for furniture and cabinetry.

Hardwood Golden Brown Termite Resistant

Mugavu

Albizia coriaria

Known for its strength and distinctive grain patterns. This East African hardwood offers excellent stability and takes finishes beautifully, making it perfect for statement pieces.

Hardwood Warm Brown High Stability

African Mahogany

Khaya senegalensis

The classic choice for premium woodwork. Rich reddish-brown color with excellent workability. Perfect for kitchen cabinetry, doors, and fine furniture requiring a distinguished appearance.

Premium Reddish Brown Fine Grain

Mvule (Iroko)

Milicia excelsa

Often called African Teak for its similar properties. Exceptional weather resistance makes it ideal for exterior doors and exposed woodwork. Ages to a beautiful silver-grey if left untreated.

Hardwood Yellow-Brown Weather Resistant

Plantation Pine

Pinus patula

A sustainable softwood option for budget-conscious projects without compromising on quality. Takes stains exceptionally well, allowing customization to match any design scheme.

Softwood Light Tone Sustainable

Eucalyptus

Eucalyptus grandis

Fast-growing and sustainable with surprising hardness. Offers excellent value with attractive grain patterns. Ideal for structural components and utility furniture.

Hardwood Pink-Brown Fast Growing

Protection & Beauty

Finish Options

The right finish protects your woodwork while enhancing its natural beauty. We offer a range of options to suit different needs and preferences.

Natural Oil

Penetrating oil that enhances grain while maintaining natural feel. Easy to maintain and repair.

Satin Lacquer

Durable topcoat with subtle sheen. Excellent protection for high-use surfaces like kitchen cabinets.

Wax Finish

Traditional hand-rubbed wax for a soft, tactile surface. Beautiful patina develops over time.

Dark Stain

Rich, deep color while preserving grain visibility. Sealed with lacquer for maximum durability.
