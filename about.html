Our Story

About BaoMbao

A modern African design-manufacturing company that converts timber into complete living systems through craft, system, and a commitment to excellence.

Craft with System

BaoMbao was founded with a simple belief: that African woodcraft could compete with the best in the world if we combined traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing systems.

Our name, "BaoMbao" (meaning "wood" in Swahili), reflects our deep connection to the material we work with every day. We see wood not just as raw material, but as the foundation for creating beautiful living environments.

Today, from our workshop on Banda Kyambogo Road, we serve homeowners, hospitality operators, developers, and design professionals across Uganda and beyond. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries our commitment to quality, beauty, and reliable delivery.

Our Mission

Transforming Wood Into Beautiful Living

We create complete living systems — kitchens, wardrobes, doors, furniture, and modular interior packages — that combine the warmth and authenticity of African woodcraft with the reliability and efficiency of structured business systems.

Our Vision

African Design Excellence

We envision a future where African design-manufacturing companies are recognized globally for their excellence. Where locally-sourced materials, skilled craftspeople, and systematic operations deliver world-class interior solutions.

What Guides Us

Our Values

Craft Excellence

Every piece we create reflects our commitment to mastering the fundamentals of woodworking. We believe that excellence is found in the details.

Reliability

We keep our promises. Our systems ensure consistent quality, accurate timelines, and clear communication throughout every project.

Material Honesty

We celebrate the natural beauty of wood. Our designs let materials express their authentic character rather than hiding behind unnecessary decoration.

Client Partnership

We see ourselves as partners in our clients' vision, not just vendors. Every project is a collaboration toward creating something meaningful.

Where It Happens

Our Workshop

Located on Banda Kyambogo Road, opposite Banda Martyrs Church, our workshop is where raw timber becomes beautiful living spaces.

The workshop houses our design station, cutting and shaping area, assembly floor, and finishing room. Modern machinery works alongside traditional hand tools, allowing us to achieve both precision and artistry.

We welcome clients to visit and see our work in progress. There's something powerful about watching your kitchen take shape on the workshop floor.
