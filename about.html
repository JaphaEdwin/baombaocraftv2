Our Story

Where Wood Meets Soul

BaoMbao Craft transforms locally sourced Ugandan wood into functional and beautiful living pieces. This is our story of design-driven manufacturing with an African perspective.

The Beginning

Crafted From Local Wood

BaoMbao Craft was born from a vision to celebrate Ugandan timber — Musambya, Mugavu, and Mahogany — transforming them into pieces that honor both modern design and African heritage. We blend minimalist, mid-century, and Japandi styles with local craftsmanship.

"Every grain tells a story. Designed in Uganda, loved everywhere. Built with purpose."

The name "BaoMbao" celebrates our foundation — the plank, the fundamental building block of every creation. Today we serve homeowners, real estate developers, and hospitality businesses across Uganda with design-driven manufacturing.

Our Journey

Milestones in Craftsmanship

2020

Workshop Foundation

BaoMbao Craft established at Banda Kyambogo Road, with focus on local timber and modern design principles.

2022

CNC Capabilities Added

Integrated modern CNC and laser craft technology while maintaining hand-finish quality and attention to detail.

2024

Expanded Collections

Launched comprehensive furniture catalogue including seating, tables, storage, bedroom, office, and outdoor collections.

2026

Today & Beyond

Serving homeowners, real estate developers, and hospitality businesses with design-driven, precision-built furniture.
